Jidai no Kigen
Sengoku jidai wa, 1336-nen kara 1573-ten made tsudzuita Muromachijidai no bunmyaku de sikai suru koto ga dekimasu. 	mMuromsachiijidai wa, Kyoto no Muromachi bakufu matawa bakufu no shougun wo tsutomreta Ashikaga-ka no touchi ni yotte teigi sa remashita. Muromachijidai ni wa, kotonaru dekigoto wo shimesu 2-tsu no sabu kikan ga fukuma rete imashita: The Nanbokuchou Period ( 1336-1392) to The Sengoku Period ( 1493-1590).
1467-nen kara 1477-nen ni kakete, koto Kyoto to sono shuhen de Ounin-Bunmei no ran ga boppatsu shi, Muromachi bakufu wa shuen wo muykaemashita.
